Visiting Professor
Center for Mathematics, Computing and Cognition. Federal University of ABC (UFABC), Brazil

I am a Ph.D. in statistics with a focus on applied statistics and financial econometrics, mainly, risk management, portfolio allocation, volatility forecasting, robust statistics, and statistical learning. I have been working on new procedures for modelling and forecasting volatility in financial time series as well as its applications to financial problems. Before I joined the Federal University of ABC, I spent some time in Brazilian companies working as a Data Science Specialist, Data Science Coordinator, and Data Scientist Consultant.

I received the y-BIS Best Paper Award (ISBIS 2016 - Barcelona, Spain), IASC-ERS Young Researchers Award (COMPSTAT 2018 - Iasi, Romania), and also the best paper Award in the 4th Latin American Conference for Statistical Computing (LACSC 2019 - Guayaquil, Ecuador).

Currently, my goal is to apply statistical learning techniques to financial problems as well as develop new volatility forecasting procedures in high-dimensional and high-frequency data.
